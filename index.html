Our Story

Your Downtown
Local Spot

Nestled in the heart of downtown San Jose, we're a warm neighborhood joint where thin‑crust pizza and expert espresso drinks share the spotlight.

Every pizza starts with hand-stretched dough made fresh daily. Our standouts — the creamy First Street Chicken Alfredo and the fire-loaded Italian — keep regulars coming back. When hunger hits hard, the Super Meat and BBQ Chicken deliver. Prefer something lighter? Our salads and pasta round it out perfectly.

Whether you're grabbing a quick slice at lunch, winding down with a cold pint, or fueling up on espresso before work — this is your downtown San Jose home base. Open every day, always welcoming.
