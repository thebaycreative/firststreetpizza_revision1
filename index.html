Our Story

Your Downtown
Local Spot

Nestled in the heart of downtown San Jose, we're a warm neighborhood joint where thin‑crust pizza and expert espresso drinks share the spotlight.

Every pizza starts with hand-stretched dough made fresh daily. Our standouts include the creamy First Street Chicken Alfredo and the fire-loaded Italian are what keep our regulars coming back.

Whether you're grabbing a quick slice at lunch, winding down with a cold pint or fueling up on espresso before work, this is your downtown San Jose's spot for all those needs and then some.
