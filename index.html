Our Story

Your Downtown
Local Spot

Nestled in the heart of downtown San Jose, we're a warm, elegant neighborhood spot where thin‑crust pizza and expert espresso drinks share the spotlight.

Our menu centers on crisp, thin crust Pizza made with premium ingredients. Standouts include the creamy Chicken Alfredo Pizza and the timeless classic Pepperoni & Cheese, alongside seasonal small plates and salads. Mornings start with pulled‑shot espressos and pastries, evenings bring craft cocktails, cold‑tapped brews, and late‑night slices.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, lingering over a cocktail, or meeting friends for a weekend espresso, we're your downtown San Jose local—open every day, always welcoming.
